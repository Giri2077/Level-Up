About Level Up

Let us help you unlock your potential.

As a group of students we have discorved and experienced at first hand the negative impact of procrastination. It has plauged our world and is the cause of stress amongst many people. We have decided to create this web app in order to combat procrastination by creating a habit tracking app that helps you to complete tasks while implementing good habits along the way. While completing tasks may seem like a chore at times, our app tries its best by giving the user a "video game" like experience by leveling up your in-app character, like video game character, everytime that you complete a task.
